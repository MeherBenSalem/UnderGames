About Back Maze

Back Maze is a survival horror game that takes inspiration from the Backrooms game. The game takes place in a dark maze filled with puzzles and challenges that players must overcome in order to escape. Players must navigate through the maze, avoiding dangers such as unknown creatures and obstacles, while solving puzzles and uncovering secrets along the way. The puzzles in Back Maze range from simple riddles to more complex logic-based puzzles that require careful thought and problem-solving skills. Players must use their wits and pay close attention to their surroundings in order to uncover clues and make their way through the maze. With a range of different puzzles, Back Maze offers a unique and challenging experience for players of all skill levels. As players progress through the game, they will encounter an increasing number of dangers, making each step more challenging and intense. The game's atmosphere is designed to be dark and creepy, with a tense soundtrack that sets the mood and immerses players in the world of Back Maze. Overall, Back Maze is a thrilling and suspenseful experience that is sure to test the nerves of even the bravest players. With its combination of challenging puzzles, atmospheric setting, and heart-pumping gameplay, Back Maze is a must-play for fans of survival horror games.
